Our Photoshop Image Clipping Path To Background Remove Services

Clipping Path is a high-end method used to remove backgrounds with extreme precision. The selection is stored as an extra “alpha channel” inside the image file, allowing the background to stay transparent permanently. You’ll receive a clean file—like a TIFF or PSD—that you can place on any background with smooth, sharp edges.
